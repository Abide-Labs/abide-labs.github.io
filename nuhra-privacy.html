Nuhra

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Nuhra ("we", "our", or "the app") is operated by Abide Labs LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information Nuhra may collect, how it is used, and what choices you have.

What Nuhra Does

Nuhra provides scripture-based guidance, reflection, and prayer responses based on situations you describe in your own words.

Information We May Process

  • User-entered reflections or prompts. When you type what you are going through, that text is processed to generate a relevant verse, reflection, and prayer.
  • Saved content on your device. If you save verses, prayers, or related content, that information may be stored locally on your device so you can view it later.
  • Purchase and subscription status. If you subscribe to Nuhra Pro, subscription and entitlement status may be processed through Apple and RevenueCat to unlock paid features.
  • Limited technical data. We may receive limited device or diagnostic information from platform and infrastructure providers to keep the app functioning and improve reliability.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account to use the app.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use cross-app tracking for advertising.

How Information Is Used

  • To generate scripture-based guidance, reflections, and prayers.
  • To save content you choose to keep.
  • To manage subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • To maintain app performance, reliability, and security.

Third-Party Services

Nuhra may rely on third-party providers for app functionality, hosting, and subscription management, including:

  • Apple App Store for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat for subscription and entitlement management
  • Expo for app infrastructure and build tooling

Please review those providers' policies for their handling of data.

Data Retention

Saved content remains available until you remove it or delete the app. Subscription records are retained according to Apple and RevenueCat policies. We may retain limited operational records as needed for support, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Children's Privacy

Nuhra is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can remove saved content from within the app or by deleting the app from your device.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we do, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact
