Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Abide Labs LLC ("Abide Labs", "we", "our") builds mobile applications and web services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how it may be used, and what choices you have.

Our Products

This policy applies to Abide Labs applications and services, including:

  • Nuhra — scripture-based reflection and guidance
  • FrugalRadar — deal discovery and local promotions
  • Think2x — AI-powered thinking companion
  • Any future applications or services we release

App-specific privacy details may be provided within individual applications. Where an app-specific policy exists, it supplements this policy.

Information We May Process

  • User-entered content. Text, reflections, or prompts you provide within our apps to receive responses or services.
  • Device and usage data. Limited device identifiers, operating system, and diagnostic information from platform providers to maintain app performance.
  • Purchase and subscription status. Entitlement data processed through Apple, Google, or RevenueCat to manage purchases and subscriptions.
  • Location data. Some features (such as FrugalRadar's local deals) may request your location. Location is only used to provide relevant results and is not stored on our servers unless you explicitly save it.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use cross-app or cross-device tracking for advertising.
  • We do not serve third-party advertisements within our applications.
  • We do not require account creation unless a specific feature requires it.

How Information Is Used

  • To deliver the features and services within our applications.
  • To generate AI-powered responses and recommendations where applicable.
  • To manage subscriptions, purchases, and entitlements.
  • To maintain app performance, reliability, and security.
  • To respond to support inquiries.

Third-Party Services

Our applications may rely on third-party providers for functionality, hosting, and operations, including:

  • Apple App Store and Google Play Store for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat for subscription and entitlement management
  • Expo for app infrastructure and build tooling
  • AI providers for generating responses where applicable

Please review those providers' policies for their handling of data.

Data Retention

Content stored within our products is retained only as long as needed to provide the feature, support the service, meet legal obligations, or protect against abuse. In some cases, content stored locally on your device may remain until you remove it or delete the app.

Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can remove saved content from within the app or by deleting the app.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple or Google account settings.
  • You can revoke location permissions through your device settings at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we do, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@abide-labs.com.
