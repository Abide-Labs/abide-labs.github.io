Common Questions

How does Nuhra work?
Nuhra lets you describe what you are going through and returns scripture-based guidance, a short reflection, and a prayer.

Do I need an account?
No. Nuhra does not require account creation for basic use.

How do subscriptions work?
Nuhra Pro unlocks unlimited guidance requests and related premium features. Purchases and renewals are managed through your Apple account.

How do I restore my purchase?
Open the paywall in the app and use the restore purchases option. If that does not work, contact us and include the device and purchase details.

How do I cancel a subscription?
You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple account subscription settings.
